Bloody mary recipe

A classic bloodymary

Learn how to make bloody mary with this easy recipe

Ingredients:

  • large handful of ice.
  • 100ml vodka.
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice, plus 2 slices to serve.
  • few shakes Worcestershire sauce.
  • few shakes Tabasco (smoked Tabasco is nice).
  • pinch celery salt
  • pinch black peppe
  • 2 celery sticks, to serve

Steps:

  1. Place the ice in a large jug
  2. Measure the vodka, tomato juice and lemon juice and pour it straight onto the ice
  3. Add 3 shakes of Worcestershire sauce and Tabasco (or more if you like it very spicy) and a pinch of celery salt and pepper. Stir until the outside of the jug feels cold, then strain the cocktail into 2 tall glasses.
  4. Top up with fresh ice, add a celery stick and lemon slice to both glasses and enjoy.
